STORIES AS NETWORK

Taking from the selected readings, Whitley decided to approach this project by sharing her personal commentaries on several areas from the reading "On Begginings" by Mary Raufle. Through the use of HTML, CSS, JS, Whitley presents the full reading in a two-column layout with bubble text icons located in specific places that when clicked leads to a pop-up of her annotations.
